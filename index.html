Friends of Shelly

Friends of Shelly is an informal and welcoming weekly support group for trans people of any gender. We meet for a couple of hours every Thursday. The first hour is usually dedicated to a group discussion focusing on a specific trans-related topic. After that, we just hang out and chat over snacks. We're graciously hosted by the very LGBTQA+ supportive The Church of St. Luke in the Fields, but the event itself is secular in nature and welcoming to people of all religious beliefs and backgrounds. Come and talk with us about hormones, family, favorite coding languages, makeup, clubbing, careers, food, NYC, etc. etc..

Wherever you are in your transition, whatever questions you have, however open you are about your gender, however uncertain you are about where you are heading, you are welcome here.
